Issue 1: Excessive AI Verbosity in Voice Mode

The AI voice system currently produces long-form responses that reduce conversational fluidity and negatively impact user engagement. The experience lacks interactivity and natural turn-taking behavior.

Goal:
Implement shorter, structured, and interactive responses with periodic pauses or prompts to maintain engagement.

Issue 2: Lack of Real-Time Speech Interruption

The system does not provide a user-controlled interruption mechanism during AI speech playback.

Goal:
Introduce a keyboard-triggered interruption feature (Spacebar) to immediately halt audio playback without disrupting application state.
